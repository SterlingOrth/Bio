About Me

Greetings! They call me, Sterling Orth. I am currently an Operations Manager @ First Watch Restaurants in Aurora, CO. I have been working in restaurants for over 15 years, but now my journey is taking me head-first into the world of coding and web design. Usually, a very quiet and reserved person, I'm super friendly, just takes me some times to 'warm up' to people. I enjoy the basics such as video games, movies (especially Marvel) and exploring new food options.

My career has been almost entirely restaurant related for my entire working career. I have lived in California, Georgia and Florida before deciding to come back to my home state of beautiful Colorado!
